Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J.Sbdul Kalam

A.P.J.Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," wan born on October 15,1931,in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu.
He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space
and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first safellite launch vehicle(SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Barat Ratna,India's highest civilian award
